Services

Premium Disc

Our goal is to bring North Alabama the finest plastic manufactuers have to offer. We have Watermelons, Paul Mcbeth Prototypes and rare Innova Classics. Premium does not have to be expensive, we will price match any online competitor.

Equipment Sales

The best disc golf equipment selection in North Alabama. With premium manufactuers such as Innova, Discraft, and MVP, we have practice baskets, bags, chairs, and more. Permanent baskets and tee-pads are avalible upon request.

Weekly League Events

In our vision for league, we want to help new players grow, while giving more advanced players a place to play competitive every week. We are currently doing the Sunday Shootout, which goes until the end of September. We hope to see you there!

Course Improvement

We want to give back as well! Improving courses and maintaining the beauty of a course is a huge thing at the Birdie Shop. Updating old baskets and constant up-keep of courses so everyone can enjoy the experience.
