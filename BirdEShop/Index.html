About Us

Yesterday,Today and Tomorrow

We started out with a box of disc and a dream to make north Alabama a discgolf destination for people around the world. Since then we have updated courses and help put new ones in the ground. Currently we have over 500 products and a larger variety than ever. Within the next year we will have a store in Huntsville so we can bring in even more disc golf manufactuers and help grow the community.

Services

Premium Disc

Our goal is to bring north Alabama the finest plastic manufactuers have to offer. We have watermelons, Paul Mcbeth Prototypes and Rare Innova Classics. Premium dose not have to be expensive, we also price match any online competitor.

Equipment Sales

The best disc golf Equipment selection in north Alabama. Premium manufactuers such as Innova, Discraft and MVP. We have practice baskets, bags, chairs, and more. Permanent baskets and tee-pads avalible upon request.

Weekly League Events

For league we wanted to help new players grow, while also giving the more advanced players a place to play competitive every week. Currently we are doing the Sunday Shootout, which goes until the end of September.

Course Improvement

We want to give back as well! Improving courses and maintaining the beauty of a course is a huge thing at the Birdie Shop. Updating old baskets and constantly maintaining the courses so everyone can enjoy the experience.
